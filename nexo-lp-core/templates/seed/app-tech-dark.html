Features

Built for Privacy,
Designed for You

Every feature is designed with your privacy and experience in mind.

End-to-End Encryption

Your messages are encrypted on your device and can only be read by the intended recipient. Not even we can read them.

Zero Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or sell your personal data. No phone number required. No email needed. Just create a username.

Voice & Video Calls

Crystal-clear encrypted voice and video calls. Group calls up to 40 participants. Works on any connection.

Self-Destructing Messages

Set timers for messages to disappear after being read. From 5 seconds to 1 week. You control the conversation.

Group Chats & Communities

Create groups up to 1,000 members. Channels, threads, file sharing, and moderation tools included.

Cross-Platform Sync

Seamlessly switch between phone, tablet, and desktop. All your messages sync in real-time across devices.
